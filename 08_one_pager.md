## The Problem

Indian college students manage their academic lives on WhatsApp. Timetables buried in group chats. Files lost forever. Attendance tracked on gut feel. The college portal exists — nobody uses it.

Every morning, millions of students ask: *what do I have today?* The answer requires scrolling, asking someone, or guessing.

This is a daily problem. Not seasonal. Every. Single. Day.

---

## The Product

**Studex** is the mobile app that answers that question — and keeps answering it, every day, for the rest of your college life.

Three features. Each one targeting a daily pain point:

**Today View** — sign up, upload a photo of your timetable, claude extracts everything. Your schedule is live in under two minutes. Clean, instant, every morning.

**Subject Folders** — all your files organized by subject, searchable, never lost. Dump everything and let AI organize it, or do it manually.

**Bunk Tracker** — your real attendance percentage, tracked by you. Claude calculates your full semester from the timetable upload and predicts exactly how many classes you can miss.

Claude is the backbone — timetable extraction, conversational onboarding, attendance intelligence, AI material search. Not an AI wrapper. Claude is the product.

Useful on day one. Works regardless of how bad your college's infrastructure is.
