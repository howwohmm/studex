### For grants, angels, accelerators, and anyone who asks "what are you building?"

## The Problem

Indian college students manage their academic lives on WhatsApp. Timetables buried in group chats. Files lost forever. Attendance tracked on gut feel. The college portal exists — nobody uses it.

Every morning, millions of students ask: *what do I have today?* The answer requires scrolling, asking someone, or guessing.

This is a daily problem. Not seasonal. Every. Single. Day.

---

## The Product

**Studex** is the mobile app that answers that question — and keeps answering it, every day, for the rest of your college life.

Three features. Each one targeting a daily pain point:

**Today View** — your schedule for today, clean and instant, every morning.
**Subject Folders** — all your files organized by subject, searchable, never lost.
**Bunk Tracker** — your real attendance percentage, tracked by you, no portal required.

Useful on day one. No network required. Works regardless of how bad your college's infrastructure is.

---

## The Market

3.52 crore undergraduate students in India. ~1.8 crore with consistent smartphone access today. $7.5 billion EdTech market — none of it built for the daily student experience.

No major player owns this space. The college portal is the competition, and it has already lost.

## The Model

Pro tier (₹49–99/month) — advanced features for students who want more.
Institutional tier (Phase 2) — colleges pay to plug in officially.

Path to ramen profitability: ~1,000 Pro users at ₹49/month. Achievable within 18 months of launch if the product earns daily habit.
